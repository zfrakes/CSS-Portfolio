About Me

Hello friends, My name is Zoey Frakes. I was born and raised in West Philadelphia. I graduated from Pennsylvania State University, where I received a bachelor of science degree in Health Policy Administration. Although I lived in Philadelphia for most of my life, I spent a year living in Washington D.C., after graduating from college. I’ve traveled a few other places but never really lived anywhere else. One of my goals in life is to travel to every continent. I want to visit every state in the United States. When I am not planning on how to travel the world; I enjoy attending plays/ musicals, watching movies, arts, and crafts, and spending time with family and friends.
